Cross the Road

If you decide to cross the river downstream, you'll come across a tribe of indigenous people. They're friendly and invite you to stay with them for a while. You learn that they know about the lost treasure, but they believe it belongs to the island and should not be taken by anyone.

Choice A

If you decide to take the treasure for yourself, you'll have to fight off the angry tribe members who see you as a thief. You manage to escape with the treasure, but you leave the island with a heavy heart and a sense of guilt for disrespecting the tribe's beliefs.

Choice B

If you decide to respect the wishes of the tribe, you'll return to the mainland empty-handed, but with a newfound respect for the people who call this island their home.

You have to choose any one option
